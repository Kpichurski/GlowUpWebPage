Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 7, 2025

Introduction

GlowUp ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application for beauty enhancement and photo transformation.

Information We Collect

Personal Information:

We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

Photos and Images:

GlowUp processes your photos locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or transmit your photos to our servers or any third-party services. All beauty enhancement and processing happens entirely on your device.

Enhanced Images:

Your enhanced photos and transformation results are stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties.

Usage Data:

We do not collect analytics data or usage statistics. GlowUp operates entirely offline on your device.

Local Storage:

The app stores your preferences locally on your device, including:

  • Enhanced photos and transformation results
  • App settings and preferences
  • Favorite filters and beauty presets
  • Before and after comparisons
  • Custom beauty enhancement settings

How We Use Your Information

We use locally stored information to:

  • Provide beauty enhancement and photo transformation services
  • Maintain your photo gallery and enhanced images
  • Remember your preferred filters and settings
  • Organize your before and after comparisons
  • Provide beauty enhancement tools and features
  • Remember your app preferences and settings

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Your photos and enhanced images remain on your device.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.

Data Security

Your photos and enhanced images remain on your device. We do not transmit or store any photo data externally. While we take steps to protect your information, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Data Retention

Your enhanced photos and app preferences are stored locally and remain there until you delete the app or reset your settings. We do not retain any personal data or photos on our servers.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Third-Party Services

GlowUp does not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting services, or cloud storage services. All processing is done locally on your device.

Beauty Enhancement Technology

Our beauty enhancement technology uses on-device machine learning models to enhance photos. No images or data are sent to external servers for processing.

App Store Compliance

GlowUp complies with both Apple's App Store and Google Play Store guidelines and privacy requirements:

  • No tracking or analytics collection
  • No data sharing with third parties
  • Local-only data processing
  • Transparent privacy practices
  • Cross-platform consistency

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (by uninstalling the app)
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns
  • Request information about our data practices

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website and updating the "Last updated" date above.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@glowup.app
  • Website: https://glowup.app
